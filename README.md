# InventorySync — Feature Summary

A quick-reference overview of what InventorySync does, written for non-technical stakeholders, clients, and resellers.

---

## 1. Goods Receiving (GR)
Receive new inventory into the warehouse with ease.
- Add stock one item at a time via a simple form
- Or upload an Excel file to receive hundreds of items at once
- Preview your upload before it's saved — catch mistakes early
- System automatically merges quantities for duplicate entries

## 2. SKU / Product Management
Build and maintain your product catalogue.
- Add individual products with material number, description, and weight
- Bulk-import products from Excel
- Pack sizes are automatically detected from the product name
- Failed imports are logged for your review

## 3. Stock Management
See and control everything in your warehouse at a glance.
- Browse your full stock list, searchable by product or material number
- Edit remarks inline without leaving the page
- Hide or show zero-quantity items
- Export your entire stock list to Excel any time

## 4. Racking & Capacity Control
Keep your racks organised and within safe limits.
- The system warns you before a rack goes over capacity
- Move all items from one rack to another in a single click (mass transfer)
- Special areas (Disposal, Damage, Tinter, etc.) are excluded from capacity checks
- Full history logged for every rack movement

## 5. Cycle Counting
Verify what's physically on the shelf against what the system says.
- Search and count rack by rack
- Autocomplete makes finding the right SKU fast
- Discrepancies are recorded and traceable

## 6. Order Management
Track customer orders from creation to delivery.
- Create orders with multiple product lines
- Edit orders inline after creation
- Cancel an order and stock is automatically returned to inventory
- Search orders by sales order number, customer, or status

## 7. Reporting & Analytics
Make data-driven decisions with built-in reports.
- Dashboard overview of key warehouse metrics
- Daily rack occupancy snapshots (automated)
- Print-ready PDF reports
- Monthly trend filtering

## 8. Security & User Access
Your data stays safe.
- Every user logs in with a secure username and password
- All sensitive actions are protected — no anonymous access
- Every stock movement records who did it and when

## 9. Mobile & Network Access
Work from anywhere on-site.
- Fully accessible from phones and tablets via browser
- Server is automatically discoverable on your local network — no IP address needed
- Colleagues just open `http://wms-server.local:5000` on any device
